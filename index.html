    Welcome to Tiina's Pokedex

    This is a simple Pokédex site featuring the first 151 Pokémon also known as Generation I.

    Search for a Pokémon by name with the search bar at the top of the page, or choose a Pokémon from the roster behind the navigation menu.

    If you use the search bar, please notice that the search bar does not recognize empty spaces or special letters. Use - instead of a space and omit all the punctuation marks.
